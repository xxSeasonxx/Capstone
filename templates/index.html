Data and Motivation

New York City, as one of the most prosperous cities in the planet, has almost 400 years of history. Numerous problem arise on daily basis and perplex the living environemnt for local resident. In response, New Yoker can file complains to the New York authorities and ask for resolutions, and this is known as the 311 services requests line. However, not all of your complains will be responsibably resolved by the NY authorities (NYA) and thus the issue grow and become severe. My website aim to help user foresee their complains result and let them make ratical decision.

Enormous Demand

High demand is the driving reason. Now day New York has 8.5 millions population and in 2015 one year alone, there was 2.5 millions complains were filed. Meaning about 1 in 4 of us actually depends on this service and awaits for New York authorities resolutions.

Authorities

Each complain is followed by an New York authority's resolution and the my data begin 2011 till now. With this unique dataset, I am able to investigate and evaulate the performance of NYA. Determine if NYA has made their effort to resolve a problem to its root

Ask the Right Question

The ultimate purpose is improve our city and to better our city we ought to see the problems. That is asking the right question. Why there is a complain? Why is the fundamental problem behind a complain. Using machine learning, we can distingusih the key aspects of a caomplain.
